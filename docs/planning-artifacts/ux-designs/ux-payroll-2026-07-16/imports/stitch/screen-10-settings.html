Settings

Configure global parameters and reference data for the salary management platform.

Outlier threshold

An employee is an outlier when their salary is more than this far from their peer median, in either direction — one finding, not two. The boundary is exact: at 20%, a distance of 19.9% does not flag and 20.1% does.

Default

At 20%, 3 employees are outliers today.

Reference tables lock

Roles and levels are fixed reference tables. Employees can only be created with these values; imports with anything else are rejected and reported.
